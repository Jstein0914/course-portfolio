Top 5 Led Zeppelin Albums

A list of the first 5 studio albums created by Led Zepellin in chronological order by year released.

  1. Led Zeppelin (1969)

    Debut album. Featured songs: Good Times Bad Times and Dazed and Confused.

  2. Led Zeppelin II (1969)

    Second album released the same year. Featured song: Whole Lotta Love.

  3. Led Zeppelin III (1970)

    Third album. Featured songs: Immigrant Song and Since I've Been Loving You.

  4. Led Zeppelin IV (1971)

    Fourth album. Featured songs: Stairway to Heaven and Rock and Roll.

  5. Houses of the Holy (1973)

    Fifth album. Featured songs: The Song Remains the Same and No Quarter.
